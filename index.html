What Data We Collect

Mantica collects minimal personal data to provide you with a seamless experience:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication via Google Sign-In or email/password
  • Display Name & Profile Photo: Retrieved from your Google account (if using Google Sign-In) to personalize your experience
  • Tarot Readings & Journal Entries: Your daily card draws, emotions, notes, and spreads are stored to track your spiritual journey
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve app performance and features

Why We Need It

We request access to your data for specific, transparent purposes:

  • Google Sign-In: To securely authenticate your account without storing passwords
  • Cloud Sync: To backup and synchronize your readings across multiple devices
  • Personalization: To display your name and provide a customized experience
  • AI Insights: To analyze your reading patterns and provide personalized spiritual guidance

How We Protect It

Your privacy and security are our top priorities:

  • Secure Storage: All data encrypted using Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore
  • Private by Default: Your readings and journal entries are visible only to you
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell or share your personal information with advertisers
  • User Control: Delete your data anytime from within the app settings
